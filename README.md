#Coffee Shop Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

Project Overview
This project involves a comprehensive analysis of sales data for a coffee shop chain across multiple locations in New York City. The dashboard provides a high-level view of monthly performance, allowing stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, product popularity, and peak operational hours to drive data-informed decisions.

Purpose
The goal of this dashboard is to answer critical business questions:
What is the revenue growth compared to the previous month?
Which store locations are performing best?
What are the peak hours and days for sales?
Which product categories and specific items drive the most value?

Tech Stack
Power BI Desktop: Lead tool for data visualization and report authoring.
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used for advanced calculations including Time Intelligence (Month-over-Month growth) and dynamic KPIs.
Power Query: Utilized for ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes to clean and shape the raw transactional data.

Key Insights & Features
Performance Metrics: Tracks Total Sales ($157K), Total Orders (33.5K), and Total Quantity Sold (48.2K) with dynamic WoW/MoM growth indicators.
Sales Trend Analysis: A daily sales chart with a calculated average line ($5,056) to identify overperforming and underperforming days.
Heatmap Visualization: A "Sales by Day | Hour" matrix that pinpoints peak morning traffic (8:00 AM – 10:00 AM), essential for staffing optimization.
Categorical Breakdown: Hierarchical view of sales from broad categories (Coffee, Tea, Bakery) down to specific product types like Barista Espresso and Brewed Chai Tea.
Geospatial Insights: Comparison of three key locations—Hell’s Kitchen, Astoria, and Lower Manhattan—all showing consistent growth above 30%.
